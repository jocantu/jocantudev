About Me

Jan 10, 2021
1 min read

I am a Software Engineer based in Tijuana, BC. I am proficient in both Frontend and Backend development. My extensive knowledge in HTML, CSS and JavaScript combined with my experience with Geographical Information Systems Technologies allows me to create outstanding solutions for your needs. Many of the solutions I provide covers the full path of any GIS project, from concept idea, through the data acquisition and preprocessing, as well as the web app development for the final user to use.
